## Privacy and Trust

- Did the local-first storage explanation feel clear?
- Did you understand what is sent to AI analysis?
- Did export, backup and local deletion feel easy to find?
- What would make you more comfortable writing a sensitive dream here?

## Capture Flow

- Was it quick enough to write a dream after waking?
- Did any controls or labels slow you down?
- Was the editor comfortable on your device?

## AI Insights

- Which AI insight felt useful or personally accurate?
- Which insight felt wrong, too generic or too confident?
- Did the personal / questionable / wrong symbol feedback make sense?

## Atlas and Reflection

- Did the Atlas make recurring motifs easier to notice?
- Were related dreams and similar dreams useful?
- Did the weekly digest feel reflective rather than diagnostic?

## Accessibility and Usability

- Could you navigate the main flow by keyboard or touch comfortably?
- Were focus states, labels and button names understandable?
- Did any text feel too small, low-contrast or crowded?

## Bugs and Friction

- What broke or behaved unexpectedly?
- What felt confusing on the first pass?
- What is the one change that would most improve your next session?
